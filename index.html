Jacob Klymenko
Welcome!
I’m Jacob Klymenko, a junior at the University of Washington Tacoma, majoring in Computer Science and Systems, and set to graduate in Autumn of 2025. My passion for software development stems from seeing friends and family excel in Software Engineering, Cloud Computing, and Web Development.
Since childhood, music has been a significant part of my life. I started playing instruments young, thanks to my parents. In middle school, I joined the school jazz band and started playing the saxophone. Joining the jazz band deepened my love for this genre, leading to my first project about jazz!
I completed this project in 2024, and it is located on the “Artists” page on this website! Essentially, it displays my, at the time, favorite jazz saxophonists with some extra information about each of them. If you would like to know more about that project, be sure to check out the “About” page which discusses it in more detail.
